There is two pointers head and tail.
Head points to the first node of the linkedlist, and the tail points to the last node array. 
Each node points to the next node of the list.

BigO in linkedList: 
Add an item to final of a linkedList is O(1)
Remove an item to the final of a linkedList is 0(n)

Add an item to the beggining of a linkedList is O(1)
Remove an item to the beggining of a linkedList is O(1)

Add an item in the middle of the linkedList is O(n)
Remove an item in the middle of the linkedList is O(n)
